About Yntra Sparks

Built by educators, for the classroom

Yntra Sparks is a STEM education company that designs activity-based learning kits for Grades 1–8. Every kit pairs a real, physical build with a teacher-ready guide, so a classroom lesson turns into an experiment students run with their own hands — no lab, no specialist, and no extra prep required. We work directly with schools to supply kits, train teachers, and track what students actually learn from each activity.
